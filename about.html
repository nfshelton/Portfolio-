Hello hello!

I am currently a junior at Carnegie Mellon University, pursuing a primary major in Creative Writing, an additional major in Human Computer Interaction, and a minor in Animation and Special Effects. I spent the past summer as a product design intern for Instagram.

I balance my studies with school activities, and volunteer work. I am co-Chair for Spirit Buggy, where Spirit races against other Carnegie Mellon teams in an intense engineering, and athletic competition. I also staff for Vision Quest, a high school youth group.

I have always been passionate about what creativity can inspire. I am driven by the process of problem solving and ideation that make visions and dreams a reality. I have been a fan of the artistic process my entire life, and the manner in which art, design and storytelling connect the world further than anticipated. I spent my childhood in London, England and have travelled to several countries for service and missions, and was fortunate enough to make life changing connections everywhere I went. This thus fired my drive and passion to connect the world through creative means.

I look forward to the day where I do the work I love, and make dreams become reality, and the future more connected.
